Low voltage landscape lighting — typically 12V — is specifically designed to be safe to install without an electrical license. In Connecticut and most states, low voltage work of this kind doesn't require a licensed electrician. The transformers and fixtures operate well below any risk threshold. That said, I'm always happy to address any concerns before we start.

No, and for low voltage landscape lighting, you don't need one. These systems run at 12 volts — far below the 120V household wiring that requires a licensed electrician. Low voltage is what makes outdoor landscape lighting safe, affordable, and the perfect fit for what I do.
